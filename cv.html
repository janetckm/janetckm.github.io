Interested in exploring the possibility in media and material by using my interdisciplinary skills in video, design and creative coding, I seek art and science as an alternative way to rethink the relationship among human, nature and machine. With more accessible technologies in XR and additive manufacturing for rapid prototyping, more people can be benefited from more user-centered designs.

Research Interests: Accessible Design, Rehabilitation, People with Special Needs
